Chaplaincy Team

At Village of Hope Hospital, we recognize that true healing encompasses both the body and spirit. Our Chaplaincy Team provides spiritual care and counseling, supporting patients through compassionate conversations and faith-based guidance. By integrating spiritual care into our health services, we aim to nurture hope and resilience in our patients and their families.
