Our Mission

At Village of Hope Hospital, our mission is to extend compassionate healthcare to all, especially to the underserved populations. Through our integrated approach, we strive to be more than just a healthcare provider; we aim to be a pillar of support and hope in our community.

Who We Are

Village of Hope Hospital (VOH) is a Christian non-profit hospital that has been actively serving the highlands of Western Kenya for several years. Our location enables us to reach patients from nearby counties including Uasin Gishu, Kakamega, and Nandi. Guided by our vision to provide holistic care, we address not just the physical health but also the emotional and spiritual well-being of those we serve.

Our Approach

Healthcare

We focus on preventative and comprehensive health services, aiming to be a one-stop-shop for our patients. Our services include health exams, urgent care, laboratory, radiology, physiotherapy, maternity, pharmacy, and soon, surgeries

Community Advancement

By mobilizing medical and educational programs, we reach out to the most vulnerable—orphans, vulnerable children, the disabled, widows, and women—empowering them towards a healthier future. We partner with local leaders and healthcare workers to enhance the quality of care in their communities

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices, ensuring long-term support and development for the communities we serve.
